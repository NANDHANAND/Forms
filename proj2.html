Itachi Uchiha

Those who cannot acknowledge themselves, will eventually fail, and Don't try to shoulder everything alone

Itachi Uchiha, a prodigy, bore the weight of a brutal mission: destroy his clan to save the village. Branded a traitor, he lived in shadow, protecting his brother from afar. Master of the Mangekyō Sharingan, his strength was matched only by his sorrow. In death, his truth emerged—Itachi was a hero hidden behind a villain’s mask.
